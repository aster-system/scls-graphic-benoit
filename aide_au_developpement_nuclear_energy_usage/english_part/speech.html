How atoms can be so powerful ?

All the things in the universe are made of atoms. An atom is composed of a central part called the nucleus of the atom. Even if this core is relatively stable, some nucleus can be broken or mixed. Einstein discovered that a broken or mixed nucleus can produce energy. Even if one nucleus does not produce a lot of energy, billions of billions of atoms can produce an heavy quantity of energy. That kind of energy is called the nuclear energy. It is used in power plants to make energy. This kind of energy represents the future of the energy. The currently used energy uses the broke of this atoms, also called nuclear fission. However, this process has some problems. Indeed, the broken nucleus became radioactive. Radioactivity is a complex phenomenous, which can be a particule as an electromagnetic wave. However, it is least dangerous than people think it is.
